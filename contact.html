About Us

We are Maria and Etienne, and together we form the dynamic duo band, Fifth of April.

While we love creating and playing our original music, we also enjoy performing a wide range of covers.

Our style primarily focuses on pop songs, singer-songwriter, and indie-rock music.

Our name, Fifth of April, is no coincidence, we were both born on the same date.
